Welcome to Skinegy!!

1 in 5
people will develop skin cancer

At Skinegy, we take the health of our users seriously.

The latest research proves that Skinegy can detect 96% of Malignant/Benign and 90% in their further subtypes . The sensitivity of general practitioners ranges from 61% and 66%, while the sensitivity of dermatologists is between 75% and 92%.

We aim to raise awareness of skin cancer and provide a regulated medical device to help individuals assess their risk and get to the doctor in time.

Getting Started

Take a photo of your skin spot

Open the Skinegy camera and take a photo of the skin spot that concerns you or manually upload a photo. Our app will assess your photo using our CE marked

Receive your risk assessment

Within 30 seconds, you'll receive one of eight risk assessments based on the risk class including malignant/benign and their further sub-categories

Track your skin spots

Securely save photos of your skin spots so you can look for changes over time. Set reminders so you can check your skin regularly.
